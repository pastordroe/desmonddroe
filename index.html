WELCOM TO DESMONDDROE

Desmond Saiwray Toweh Droe was borned unto Mrs.Seeweyee Droe and David Tweh Droe on August 8, 1986. He was borned in Zwedru City, Grand Gedeh County, Republic of Liberia, West Africa. Rev.Droe begin his primary education at Trade Town Cooperative School once located in on 18th Street, Sinkor, Monrovia, Liberia. He graduated from the William V.S.Tubman High School and purued with Religious Studies at West Africa School of Missiom and Theology (WASMAT)and earned Bachelor of Religious Education (BRE) and also went to Trinity Bible College and School of Business.
